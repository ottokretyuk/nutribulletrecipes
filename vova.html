NutriBullet Recipes - Smoothie Recipes (Diabetics)

This NutriBullet Diabetic diet app brings you Diabetic Friendly Smoothie recipes that can help in regulating blood sugar and supply the body with a more stable source of energy.

People with diabetes or even those that are considered pre-diabetes should keep their blood sugar levels within the normal range to prevent having complications. A person may experience several symptoms such as hunger, sweating, blurred vision, nausea, headache, and lethargy when blood sugar is either very low or very high. These symptoms can lead to a more serious or life-threatening condition.


This Smoothie Recipes app aims to help people with diabetes in managing their blood sugar levels by using diabetic diet recipes made with natural and fresh ingredients such as fruits and vegetables, seeds and nuts, dairy, tea, and water.
